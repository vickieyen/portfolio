Saltbae (Hackathon)

Saltbae is an incremental clicking game, where users can either shake touch screen laptops OR click 'shake' to help the popular internet meme, Saltbae shake the optimal amount of salt onto different foods. Earn more tips when you are closer to the optimal amount. Level up and try to get on the leaderboard!
